The Pumpkin is Published

June 17th, 2025

It's been a long journey but The Pumpkin with a Crack in It is finally a real book! I started out with an old character courtesy of my 7-year-old self, and a dream of writing a story for my little one to enjoy.

Half a year and much learning later, The Pumpkin with a Crack in It is officially published. I'm so grateful to my wife for all her love and support (and marketing prowess, which I severely lack) and to the extremely talented Hema Gami for bringing Pumpkin to life with her beautiful illustrations.

The book is available on Amazon and IngramSpark, and will be in select local bookstores soon. I'm so excited to share this story with you!
