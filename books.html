The Pumpkin with a Crack in It

ISBN-13: 9798992489620

Ages: 2-6

Pumpkin is the most perfect gourd on the farm. But when he lets it get to his head, he is in for a hard lesson! Join Pumpkin and his friends as he learns about the importance of character and inner beauty.

Beautifully illustrated and written to entertain and teach, The Pumpkin with a Crack in It is a wonderfully relatable story for children (with some fun puns for the parents too).
